Dog

Dogs have been long regarded as Man's Best Friend

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Lifepsan: 10-13 years
  • Daily Sleep: 12-14 hours
  • Height: 2.5-42 in

Did you know dogs can understand up to 250 words? That's impressive to say the least. It's no wonder they have strong reactions to our commands and have the distinction of being man's best friend. They even have their own language that we can interpret that danger is near.
